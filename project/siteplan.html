Sucre's Savory & Styles - Site Plan

Site Purpose

Sucre's Savory & Styles is an online thrift store dedicated to displaying and selling affordable, eco-friendly female thrift wears. The website aims to provide a seamless shopping experience, where visitors can browse items, view detailed descriptions, add items to a cart, and check out. Additionally, it promotes sustainable fashion choices with a focus on affordability, cleanliness, and eco-friendliness.

Color Schema

  • Yellow (#FFD700): Used for headings and accents, representing affordability and happiness.
  • Green (#228B22): Background color for eco-friendly focus, seen in section backgrounds and icons.
  • White (#FFFFFF): Body background for a clean, fresh look.

Typography

  • Header Font: Arial, sans-serif (used for site headings and main titles for a bold, modern look).
  • Body Font: Verdana, sans-serif (used for body text to ensure readability and simplicity).
